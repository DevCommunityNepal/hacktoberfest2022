HacktoberFest 2022

What is HacktoberFest 2022?

Hacktoberfest isn't all about code. Anyone who writes, designs, tests, mentors, or organizes offers much needed support for open-source projects.

How to participate in HacktoberFest 2022?

Everyone can contribute on HacktoberFest 2022. First step you have go to https://hacktoberfest.com/ and register and open account. To register you should have a Github Account. After creating account you can start contributing on open source projects.

How do I get my free T shirt from Hacktoberfest?

Register at hacktoberfest.com. Then you need to make 4 valid pull requests to a public repo. That's all! And after selection process if you get selected you will receive the swags.

How many pull request are required for Hacktoberfest 2022?

At least four of your pull requests must be merged or accepted in the respective repositories. Your pull request must be sent to repositories that have the topic “Hacktoberfest” in it, or it must be labeled as “Hacktoberfest-accepted“.

Who is maintainer in Hacktoberfest?

There are two ways to participate in Hacktoberfest: as a contributor or as a maintainer. A contributor is someone that helps open source projects resolve issues they have opened. Whereas a maintainer manages an open source project and presents issues that they need help with.
